## Actionable Timeline (10 Weeks)

    Weeks 1-2: Foundation. NO DAA. Only focus on C++ STL and implementing all major Data Structures. Follow Striver's basic DS videos.

    Weeks 3-4: Unit I. Watch Abdul Bari on notations and sorting. Implement Merge Sort, Quick Sort, and Binary Search. Solve 10-15 related problems on GFG/LeetCode.

    Weeks 5-6: Unit II. Watch Abdul Bari on Greedy algorithms. Implement Dijkstra's, Prim's, and Kruskal's. Understand the Fractional Knapsack.

    Weeks 7-8: Unit III. This is the toughest unit. Give it time. Watch Aditya Verma's DP playlists. Start with the basics and solve at least 15-20 DP problems, starting from the easiest ones.

    Week 9: Unit IV. Watch videos on Backtracking (N-Queens). Then, watch a conceptual video explaining P vs NP.

    Week 10: Unit V & Revision. Watch high-level explainers for the advanced topics. Spend the rest of the time revising all previous units and solving mixed problems.
